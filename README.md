# Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard is an interactive data analytics project created using Tableau.
This dashboard helps analyze business performance by tracking sales, profit, customer segments, product categories, and regional performance.
The project provides meaningful insights through KPI cards, charts, filters, and trend analysis. It helps businesses identify high-performing regions, profitable categories, and sales growth patterns for better decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

Main Objectives of the Project

### 1️⃣ Analyze Overall Sales Performance

•	Monitor total sales revenue 

•	Track sales trends over time 

•	Identify peak sales periods 

### 2️⃣ Analyze Profitability

•	Measure total profit 

•	Compare profit across regions and categories 

•	Identify loss-making areas 

### 3️⃣ Understand Customer Segments

•	Analyze Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments 

•	Identify which segment contributes highest revenue 

### 4️⃣ Category-wise Performance Analysis

•	Compare sales and profit across categories 

•	Identify top-performing products/categories 

### 5️⃣ Regional Analysis

•	Compare sales and profit by region 

•	Identify best-performing regions 

### 6️⃣ Improve Business Decision Making

•	Provide interactive insights using filters and dashboards 

•	Help stakeholders make data-driven decisions

## 📈 Analytics Types Insights

### 1️⃣ Descriptive Analytics — What Happened?

- Total sales increased by 23.6% compared to previous year.

- Technology category contributed 33.6% of total sales.

- West region generated the highest profit contribution of 34%.

### 2️⃣ Diagnostic Analytics — Why Did It Happen?

- Sales increased mainly due to higher Technology product demand.

- West region performed better because of higher customer orders and profit margins.

- Standard Class shipping contributed more orders because it is cost-effective.

### 3️⃣ Predictive Analytics — What May Happen?

- If current growth continues, next quarter sales may increase by approximately 15–20%.

- Technology category is expected to remain the top-performing category.

- Mobile and online orders are likely to grow further in future periods.

### 4️⃣ Prescriptive Analytics — What Should Be Done?

- Increase focus on Technology products to improve revenue.

- Improve marketing strategies in low-performing regions like North.

- Offer discounts on high-profit products to increase customer engagement.

- Optimize shipping strategies to reduce delivery costs and improve profit margins.
